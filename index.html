CONNEXION

All Branches Team of 2 EEE / ECE Focus

Connexion is a dynamic, knowledge-driven technical event that tests participants' analytical thinking, problem-solving skills, and core understanding of electrical concepts.

Through multiple competitive rounds, teams engage in quizzes, design-based identification tasks, and component decoding challenges.

Each stage progressively enhances practical knowledge of electrical components, theories, and their applications.

Blending learning with competition, Connexion creates a professional yet exciting platform for aspiring electrical engineers.

// Event Rounds
ROUND 01
Technical Quiz
Teams engage in rapid-fire quizzes testing core understanding of electrical concepts and theory.
ROUND 02
Design-Based Identification
Design-based identification tasks where teams interpret circuit diagrams and component layouts.
ROUND 03
Component Decoding
Advanced component decoding challenges testing practical knowledge of electrical components and their applications.

ANALOG ARENA

EEE / ECE Team of 2 Hands-on

Analog Arena is a hands-on technical competition focused on real-time circuit analysis, component selection, and analog circuit implementation.

Participants interpret circuit diagrams, choose appropriate components, and construct functional circuits on breadboards under time constraints.

Advanced rounds challenge teams to modify and optimize circuits to meet specific performance criteria such as voltage regulation and efficiency.

By simulating real engineering workflows, the event strengthens practical skills, troubleshooting ability, and technical confidence in analog electronics.

// Event Rounds
ROUND 01
Circuit Analysis & Component Selection
Teams interpret circuit diagrams and select appropriate components for the given task under time pressure.
ROUND 02
Breadboard Construction
Build functional circuits on breadboards based on provided specifications, demonstrating hands-on practical skills.
ROUND 03
Optimization Challenge
Modify and optimize existing circuits to meet specific performance criteria such as voltage regulation and efficiency targets.

SPARK THE BRAIN

All Branches Individual Multi-Level Quiz

Spark the Brain is a dynamic, knowledge-driven technical quiz designed to enhance analytical thinking and practical problem-solving skills.

The event progresses through multiple levels, beginning with a fundamental MCQ round that tests core electrical concepts.

Participants then tackle real-life visual fault identification challenges that assess observation and applied technical knowledge.

The final round emphasizes advanced reasoning and confident decision-making, creating an engaging blend of learning and competition.

LEVEL 02
Visual Fault Identification
Real-life visual fault identification challenges assessing observation skills and applied technical knowledge.
LEVEL 03
Advanced Reasoning
Advanced reasoning and decision-making under pressure — the final test of analytical thinking and technical confidence.

PAPER PRESENTATION

All Branches Team of 2–3 Research

Paper Presentation is a technical event that provides a platform for students to showcase their research ideas, innovative concepts, and analytical skills.

Participants are invited to present original research papers, case studies, technical reviews, or innovative project ideas in their respective domains.

This event aims to encourage critical thinking, technical knowledge enhancement, communication skills, and research aptitude among students. Participants will present their papers before a panel of judges and audience, followed by a question-and-answer session.

The event promotes knowledge sharing, creativity, and confidence, helping students gain exposure to academic and industry-level presentation standards.

// Event Format
PHASE 01
Paper Submission & Screening
Submit original research papers, case studies, or technical reviews. Shortlisted teams proceed to the presentation stage.
PHASE 02
Presentation Before Panel
Present your paper before a panel of expert judges and audience. Showcase clarity, depth, and innovation in your work.
PHASE 03
Q&A Session
Defend your research in a rigorous question-and-answer session evaluating depth of understanding and conceptual clarity.

HUMAN LUDO

Open to All Team of 4 High Energy

Human Ludo is a life-size, high-energy version of the classic Ludo game where participants become the tokens on a giant board.

Four teams compete by rolling a dice and moving across challenge-based squares that test their knowledge and teamwork.

Each box contains a task that must be completed within a time limit to progress further.

Blending strategy, coordination, and fun, the event creates an engaging and competitive atmosphere for all participants.

// How It Works
STEP 01
Roll the Dice
Each team rolls to move their human token across the giant life-size board. Strategy begins from the very first move.
STEP 02
Complete the Challenge
Land on a square? Complete the challenge task within the time limit to stay on the board and earn points.
STEP 03
Race to Win
Navigate snakes and ladders, outmaneuver rivals, and be the first team to reach the finish. Teamwork wins!

D²

Open to All Duo Double Dare

This event is a multi-phase investigative challenge that tests analytical reasoning, technical skills, and teamwork under pressure.

Participants analyze case files, examine evidence, and develop logical conclusions to identify suspects and motives.

The competition advances to a technical electrical debugging round where teams diagnose and solve circuit-based problems.

In the final stage, teams synthesize all evidence to present a well-reasoned verdict, showcasing critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

Case File Analysis
Analyze case files and examine evidence to develop logical conclusions — identify suspects and build your theory.
PHASE 02
Technical Debugging
Diagnose and solve circuit-based electrical problems in a hands-on debugging round under time pressure.
PHASE 03
Final Verdict
Synthesize all evidence and present a well-reasoned verdict — the ultimate test of critical thinking and communication.

BLACKOUT SURVIVAL

Open to All Team of 3 Challenge-Based

The Blackout Survival Challenge is a fun and interactive classroom event that tests teamwork, creativity, and decision-making under simulated power failure situations.

Participants must think quickly, adapt to unexpected challenges, and work together to succeed when the lights go out.

Blending strategy and performance, this challenge encourages collaboration, confidence, and quick thinking — because when the lights go out, true teamwork shines. ⚡

// Event Rounds
ROUND 01 — 🎲
Power Grid Rush
Teams compete on a 30-box Snake and Ladder styled grid, completing blackout-themed tasks to earn points. Ladders boost progress with bonus marks, while snakes cause setbacks with deductions.
ROUND 02 — 🎭
Current Confusion
Teams perform a role play based on a real-life blackout scenario, showcasing creativity, communication, and problem-solving skills in a dramatic and entertaining format.
